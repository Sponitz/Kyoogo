About Us

A little bit about who we are, and what we do

Welcome to Kyoogo

Kyoogo is a Twin Cities based startup corporation striving to provide the highest level of service for IT solutions. Being founded in 2010, we have experience in the latest techniques to make sure every challenge that our customers bring to us - is a success.

We offer technical IT support and Computer Repair to consumers and small to mid-sized businesses in the Minneapolis and St. Paul area. Building customized computers and servers for consumers and businesses to increase productivity and overall workflow. We provide services from Windows operating system installation, to virus removal, to malware removal and even hard drive data recovery. We also provide wired and wireless networking solutions for our business clients and consumers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to give the highest quality in customized, professional service to our clients, so that we not only meet but exceed their expectations. We strive to be a "one-stop-shop" for all IT services.

We are dedicated to offering fair and honest technical support at fair and honest prices. We will never charge our customers for service if no service is provided. If we do not fix it, there is no charge. Ever. Our knowledgeable and service oriented staff are experienced in their field and they use their expertise to provide effiecient service. Our business is to help you get on with yours.
